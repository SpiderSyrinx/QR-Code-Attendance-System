Privacy Policy

At Presentech, we respect your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information in our QR Code Attendance system.

Information We Collect

  • First Name, Middle Name, Last Name
  • Contact Number and Email
  • Course / Section and Year / Grade
  • QR Code data for attendance tracking

How We Use Your Information

  • To create your student profile and generate QR codes.
  • To track attendance when scanned by teachers.
  • To improve the functionality and security of the system.

Data Protection

Your personal information is stored locally in your browser's localStorage and is only accessible when you log in. It is not shared with third parties.

Your Rights

You can log out at any time to prevent others from accessing your profile. You can also request your data to be removed by clearing your browser's local storage.
